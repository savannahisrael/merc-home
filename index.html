About Us We supply organic, fair trade, and sustainably sourced botanical products to the greater Eugene community and the world travelers who find themselves here. Explore our wellness apothecary, complete with over 200 organic herbs and spices, 70 varieties of loose-leaf tea, 140 pure essential oils and hydrosols, and a plethora of house made herbal tinctures and natural skin care products.

An Organic Goodness Marketplace

Have questions about our products? Want to get crafty with a DIY project? We're here to help! Visit us to see, smell, and touch your favorite botanical ingredients or to ask our knowledgeable staff for some guidance.
Our store is located in the Farmers' Union Marketplace, a collection of buildings on the National Register of Historic Places. The Marketplace only accepts businesses with a sustainability focus, and we are honored to join this community of like-minded shops in Eugene! Sip on some coffee or tea from the Farmers Union Coffee Roasters, shop for plants and quirky kitchen gadgets at Down to Earth, and peruse some antiques at Oak Street Vintage.
What You Will Find

The Herb & Spice Wall

Peruse hundreds of organic herbs, spices, and herbal powders! Our interactive herb wall allows you to use your senses to further explore each botanical.

Well stocked with classic herbal ingredients along with the harder-to-find and more obscure ingredients, we are prepared to support all your herbal needs.

Aromatherapy

With over 130 essential oils, various hydrosols, aroma blends, and room sprays, our aromatherapy section is sure to have something for everyone.

Explore green cleaning options, on-the-go perfumery, or essential oil diffusers that create ambiance and distribute fresh scents to your home, office, or car.

NATURAL BATH & BODY CARE

All of our natural body care offerings must meet our quality and purity standards while emphasizing the importance of using organic ingredients.

With an array of skin care treatments, plant-based hair products, and more, we are sure you will be delighted by our natural, gentle, and effective bath & body offerings.

The Wonderful World of Teas

The Mountain Rose Herbs Mercantile is stocked with the largest selection of organic loose-leaf tea in the Eugene area. Explore our vast selection of black, green, white, and chai tea varieties. Or if you prefer caffeine-free options, you'll love our red and house-blended herbal teas.

Offered in reusable tea tins, and a selection of bulk sizes, this is a dream destination for tea lovers, or those trying to find a gift for the tea enthusiast in their life.

The Mercantile is a cultural destination for all your natural product needs. Explore a myriad of herbs, spices, tea blends, essential oils, DIY supplies, organic body and self-care items, books, and much more!

Housed in the historic Famers' Union Marketplace, our flagship brick and mortar store is a complete community apothecary in downtown Eugene.

JOURNAL & CATALOG

Don't see what you need in the retail store?

Explore many more offerings in the Mountain Rose Herbs Photo Journal & Catalog or visit our online store at wwww.mountainroseherbs.com.

BOOKS

We offer a wealth of resources for natural health and plant-related passions.

You'll find books on medicinal mushrooms, herbalism, foraging, gardening, cooking, body care, and so much more!

DIY INGREDIENTS & SUPPLIES

From butters and waxes to clays and carrier oils, we offer everything you need to create the perfect natural body care recipes.

Need some inspiration or assistance? Just to inquire with one of our friendly and knowledgeable team members.

GIFTS

The Mountain Rose Herbs Mercantile offers the perfect variety of products for gifts. Explore locally made crafts, essential oil gift sets, put together a DIY kit with a recipe from our blog, or create your very own themed gift with a beautiful Mountain Rose Herbs Mercantile gift box.

Not sure what to get? We also offer gift certificates for any occasion.

Principles We put people, plants, and planet before profit with everything we do: from sourcing organic botanicals, to cleaning up the wetlands and rivers near our campus, to becoming the first zero-waste-certified business in Oregon. It's not always easy being first, but that doesn't stop us from continually looking for ways to do business while leaving the lightest possible footprint on this Earth. Thank you for helping us fund environmental nonprofits, encourage our employees to bike and walk to work, and preserve the beauty of the nature we were given.
