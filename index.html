AN ORGANIC GOODNESS MARKETPLACE

We supply organic, fair trade, and sustainably sourced botanical products to the Eugene community and beyond! Shop over 275 herbs and spices, 70 varieties of tea, 140 essential oils and hydrosols, and a plethora of herbal tinctures, artisan salts, culinary oils, natural skin care products, soap making supplies, and so much more for your health and harmony.

Have questions about our products? Want to get crafty with a DIY project? We’re here to help! Visit us to see, smell, and touch your favorite botanical ingredients. Explore the shop or place an order from our website at one of our convenient kiosks.

Our store is located in the Farmers' Union Marketplace, a collection of buildings on the National Register of Historic Places. The Marketplace only accepts businesses with a sustainability focus, and we are honored to join this community of like-minded shops in Eugene!

We always put people, plants, and planet over profit. One of the ways we make this mission a reality is to support local organizations that work to protect Oregon’s ecosystems. Each time you bring your own shopping bag, we will make a donation to one of the nonprofit partners of your choice! When you support us, we support them.
